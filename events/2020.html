Take The Leap

  1. Home
  2. Events

The first ever event organized by TEDxPVGCOET, 'Take The Leap!', was themed aptly. Speakers from different backgrounds shared ideas and experiences pertaining to the theme, that motivated and inspired the audience. In its pneuma, taking the leap is all about making the best of the freedom and liberty given to us, giving ourselves the chance to make mistakes and grow, paving the way for others to give their passion a chance, and throwing in all we have got and await all that life has in store for us. So, open your mind, unbound your thoughts, and let's together, Take the leap!
